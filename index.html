05About

About

Liam Jansson is a videographer, editor and creative based in Berlin. His work blends raw, street-level authenticity with a graffiti-influenced visual language — from quick-turnaround event coverage to full-scale brand films.

He brings instinct and precision to every project: a director's eye on set, a colorist's patience in the suite. Available worldwide.
